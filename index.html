About Rambo Data

Rambo Data is a fast and reliable data bundle reseller in Ghana. We provide instant top-ups for MTN, Telecel, and AirtelTigo subscribers across the country — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.


  • Instant bundle delivery after payment
  • All major networks supported
  • Secure payment via DigiMall
  • Order history tracking
  • Available 24/7

How to Buy

  • Go to "Buy Data" in the sidebar
  • Select your network (MTN, Telecel, AirtelTigo)
  • Click the bundle you want
  • Enter the recipient's mobile number
  • Click "Pay & Get Data" — you'll be sent to a secure payment page
  • Once paid, data is delivered within seconds
